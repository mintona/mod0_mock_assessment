Making a Sandwich

1 Prepare your workspace
  1.1 Cutting board
    1.1.1 Open cabinet
      1.1.1.1 Remove cutting board
    1.1.2 Set cutting board on counter

  1.2 Knife
    1.2.1 Open drawer
      1.2.1.1 Remove knife from drawer
    1.2.2 Set knife next to cutting board

2 Prepare the ingredients
  2.1 Bread
    2.1.1 Get bread from bread box
      2.1.1.1 Open bread bag
        2.1.1.1.1 Take the closure tab off the bag
        2.1.1.1.2 Untwist bag to expose the opening
      2.1.1.2 Remove 2 slices of bread simultaneously
        2.1.1.2.1 Reach in open bag and take out 2 slices of bread
    2.1.2 Place the slices of bread side by side on the cutting board
      2.1.2.1 Lay the slices of bread flat on the cutting board

  2.2 Mayo
    2.2.1 Get out mayo from refrigerator
      2.2.1.1 Open mayo jar
        2.2.1.1.1 Untwist the lid to remove from jar
        2.2.1.1.2 Set the lid aside
    2.2.2 Relocate the open mayo jar next to the cutting board

  2.3 Turkey
    2.3.1 Get out turkey from refrigerator
      2.3.1.1 Open the bag of turkey
      2.3.1.2 Remove desired amount of turkey from the bag
        2.3.1.2.1 Reach into bag and take 3-4 oz of turkey out
    2.3.2 Place turkey on cutting board

  2.4 Cheese
    2.4.1 Get out cheese from refrigerator
      2.4.1.1 Open the bag of cheese
      2.4.1.2 Remove one slice of cheese
        2.4.1.2.1 Reach into bag and take out once slice of cheese
    2.4.2 Place cheese on cutting board

3 Assemble the sandwich
  3.1 Spread the mayo on the face up side of each slice of bread
    3.1.1 Scoop 1 tbsp of mayo out of the jar with the knife
      3.1.1.1 Pick-up the knife from the counter
      3.1.1.2 Put the knife into the jar of mayo
      3.1.1.3 Scoop mayo onto the knife
    3.1.2 Transfer the mayo to once slice of bread
      3.1.2.1 Move the knife with the mayo over to the slice of bread
      3.1.2.2 Dump the mayo from the knife onto the bread
    3.1.3 Spread the mayo with the knife in an even layer
      3.1.3.1 Move the knife back and forth to move the mayo around the bread
    3.1.4 Repeat steps for the 2nd slice
  3.2 Place the cheese on top of one slice of bread
    3.2.1 Pick-up the cheese from the cutting board
    3.2.2 Put the cheese down on one slice of bread
  3.3 Place the turkey on top of the cheese
    3.3.1 Pick-up the turkey from the cutting board
    3.3.2 Put the turkey down on top of the cheese
  3.4 Place the other slice of bread mayo side down on top of the cheese
    3.4.1 Pick-up the bare slice of bread from the cutting board
      3.4.1.1 Flip the bread 180 deg so it is mayo side down
      3.4.1.2 Line the bread up so it matches the orientation of the bottom slice of bread
    3.4.2 Place the bread on top of the cheese
